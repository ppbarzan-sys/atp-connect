The Opportunity

Millions of students.
Two barriers, reinforcing each other.

Most schools can't afford permanent labs. Most systems don't have enough qualified STEM teachers. Without labs, trained teachers struggle. Without teachers, even good labs go unused. ATP Connect solves both — at once.

◈

No infrastructure

Labs require space, water, power, ventilation, safety certification, and capex most schools don't have.

✕

No expert teachers

Training one STEM specialist takes years. Scaling that to every classroom is, by itself, impossible.

Value Proposition

Different value for every stakeholder.

For Schools

  • No lab? No problem. Any classroom becomes a science lab.
  • Not a STEM expert? Gali provides real-time expert support.
  • Passive students? Hands-on, gamified — engagement by design.
  • One-size-fits-all? Groups can work on different subjects in parallel.

For Governments

  • Curriculum-aligned. Complements — doesn't replace — existing programs.
  • Scalable. AI tutoring reaches thousands of classrooms at near-zero marginal cost.
  • Measurable. Analytics track outcomes, completion, and engagement.
  • Cost-effective. Expertise embedded in the platform drops per-student cost dramatically.

For Partners

  • Sustainable model. Recurring SaaS plus hardware sales.
  • Proven tech. Hardware made in Italy; software built on modern stacks.
  • Global reach. Multilingual, curriculum-agnostic by design.
  • Daily engagement. Value extends far beyond scheduled lab hours.
